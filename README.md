## 📊 Prediction Methodology

### Strength Rating Formula
```
Base Rating  = 60% × Historical_Win_Rate_Normalized
             + 40% × Pre-tournament_Market_Probability

After each round:
Updated Rating = Base × R1_form × R2_form × R3_form
(weighted: R1=20%, R2=35%, R3=45% — most recent games matter most)

Market Calibration (knockout stage):
Final Rating = 60% × Statistical_Model + 40% × FanDuel/Polymarket_Implied_Prob
```

### Monte Carlo Simulation
- **10,000 full tournament simulations** from Round of 32 to Final
- Goals sampled from **Poisson distribution** per team's expected goals
- Knockout tiebreaker: stronger team wins ET/Pens with probability proportional to rating gap
- Result: championship win%, semifinal%, quarterfinal%, R16% for every team
